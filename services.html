Services Provided By Us

We offer a wide range of medical services to meet the diverse needs of our patients.


Our Services
  • Emergency Care: Our 24/7 emergency department is ready to provide immediate care for life-threatening conditions and injuries.
  • Outpatient Services: We offer convenient outpatient services for diagnostics, treatments, and follow-up care, including lab tests, physical therapy, and more.
  • Surgery: Our surgical team specializes in various procedures, from minimally invasive surgeries to complex operations, ensuring you receive the best care with minimal recovery time.
  • Maternity and Neonatal Care: Our maternity unit provides comprehensive care for expecting mothers and their newborns, including prenatal classes, labor and delivery, and postpartum support.
  • Radiology and Imaging: We offer advanced imaging services, such as X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans, to assist in accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.
  • Pharmacy: Our in-house pharmacy provides prescription medications, over-the-counter drugs, and personalized consultation with pharmacists.
  • Specialty Clinics: We have a range of specialty clinics, including cardiology, oncology, orthopedics, and more, staffed by experts in each field.
